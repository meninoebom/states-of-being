Song Blender

Move, and the music moves with you.

Your camera watches how you move, and a song bends to match. It rises as you reach, softens as you settle, and drifts as you go still. Think of it as a duet between your body and the sound.

  1. 1
    Allow the camera It is how the music sees you.
  2. 2
    Step back Give the camera room to see your whole body.
  3. 3
    Move Reach, sway, or hold still, and listen for the change.

Everything runs in your browser. Pose detection happens on your own device, and your camera feed is never uploaded, recorded, or sent anywhere.

This experience needs a browser that can use your camera, like Chrome, Edge, or Safari. It works best on a laptop or desktop with room to move.
