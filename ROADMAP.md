# States of Being — Song Blender Roadmap

## Product Vision

A fun, widely available web tool where people explore a "song space" using their webcam. Movement drives how songs blend and transform in real time — a game-like biofeedback experience.

**Target audience (v1):** Adults looking for a novel, playful experience
**Future direction:** Kid-oriented versions

## Tiers

### Free Tier
- Full webcam + movement experience
- Curated library of Brandon's songs pre-loaded in the blender
- Songs are always available — no setup, just play

### Premium (Paid)
- Upload your own songs into the blender
- Generate new songs (details TBD)
- Custom song spaces

## Current Phase: Go Maximal on Blender Features

The song blender is the core product surface. Priority is making the blending experience as rich, fun, and surprising as possible with the curated song library before gating premium features.
